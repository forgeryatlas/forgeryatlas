To His Excellency Fuad Pasha
Minister of Foreign Affairs, etc., etc., etc.

Consulate General of the Sublime Porte in the Cyclades
No. P. 527
Syra, 11 January 1859

Monseigneur,

I have the honor to transmit to Your Excellency the enclosed information, which has been given to me by a person passing through Syra concerning the individuals named Giuseppe Civicov, Pittz, Giuseppe Lopez, and Nicolo Feranti, who, it is said, are engaged in the manufacture of counterfeit money in Constantinople.

As this information may be of great value to the Ministry of Police in putting them on the trail of the criminals engaged in this unlawful activity, I considered it my duty, as an official of the Imperial Government, to bring it immediately to the attention of Your Excellency.

I have the honor to be, with the deepest respect,
Monseigneur, of Your Excellency,
your very humble and very obedient servant,

G. S. Robert
