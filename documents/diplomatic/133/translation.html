Turin, March 20, 1862

No. G. 1390
No. G. 416

To His Highness Aali Pasha, Minister of Foreign Affairs, Constantinople

Your Highness,

The Minister of Foreign Affairs of Italy has just sent me a note in which he renews, on behalf of the Court of Appeal of Messina, his requests in order to obtain a reply to the request for documents formulated in a letter rogatory of the said Court, concerning the counterfeiters of kaimes whom the Imperial Government has had arrested in Messina; a letter rogatory which I transmitted to the Imperial Ministry in my report of December 12 last (No. 1284/361).

In hastening to transmit herewith a copy of this note to Your Highness, I beg you to kindly issue orders so that the documents in question may be completed without further delay and sent to me as soon as possible.

Your Highness will understand how such a delay, if prolonged, could produce adverse effects and harm the result we seek to achieve in prosecuting the guilty parties; I therefore hope that you will enable me to satisfy, as soon as possible, the request that has just been conveyed to me.

Deign, Your Highness, to accept the assurances of my highest and most respectful consideration, with which I have the honor to remain,

Your Highness’s most humble most obedient servant

Rüstem
