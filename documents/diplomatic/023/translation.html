GENERAL ADMINISTRATION – TELEGRAPH LINES
Telegraphic Dispatch

Turin, 28 May 1858
No. 3170

To His Excellency Mahmoud Pasha, Minister of Foreign Affairs
Constantinople

I urgently request Your Excellency to have sent to me, without further delay, all the documents that are to serve in proving the guilt of Bondesio, arrested in Turin at your request, for otherwise I will soon no longer be able to prevent his release.

Rustem

Certified true copy
[signature illegible]
