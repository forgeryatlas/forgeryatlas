To His Highness Aali Pasha to Rüstem Bey at Turin

15 May 1861

No. 5098/110

Mr. Chargé d’Affaires,

I have received your report dated 25 April, No. 1018/225, enclosing the letter from the Prosecutor General at the Court of Appeal of Bologna concerning the fixing of 1 May of the current month as the date of the trial of the counterfeiters in question.

I am convinced that the reasons which led you not to appear as a civil party in the proceedings are such as to deserve consideration.

Therefore, I do not hesitate to approve the decision you have taken in this regard.

Please accept, etc.
