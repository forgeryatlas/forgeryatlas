Turin, March 17, 1861

Rüstem Bey Ottoman Chargé d’Affaires in Turin

Mr. Chargé d’Affaires,

By my note of December 6 last, I transmitted to you a letter rogatory which the Court of Appeal of Messina had addressed to the judicial authorities in Constantinople, with the aim of obtaining new information necessary to expedite the proceedings against the counterfeiters of kaimes.

The King’s Public Prosecutor attached to the said Court having addressed to me further requests to obtain a reply to the said letter rogatory, I beg you, Sir, to kindly use your good offices with the competent authority and to enable me to satisfy, as soon as possible, the request of the Court of Appeal of Messina, so that it may be able to decide on the fate of the detainees.

Please accept, etc. The Minister
(Signed) Melegari
