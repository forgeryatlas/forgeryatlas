Naples, October 17, 1859
No. 279

Excellency,

I have the honor to acknowledge receipt of Your Excellency’s note No. 3150 of October 5. As soon as I received the note which Your Excellency was kind enough to send me on July 20 last (No. 2789), I hastened to forward to Mr. Chevalier Giri, Director of Police of Bologna, the various documents that had reached me, namely:

1° extracts from the interrogations undergone by the detainees in Constantinople;
2° translations of the articles of both the former and the new Penal Code concerning counterfeiters;
3° extracts from the register concerning one Ulysse Baldini, arrested in Bologna on the representations of Mr. Tkiadès;
4° Finally, three photographs, which Mr. Giri was requested to have shown to the detainees in Bologna in order to collect their statements and to send them directly to Constantinople, accompanied by a report of those statements.

However, since the beginning of the trial, political events that occurred in Bologna obliged Mr. Giri, like all the principal officials, to leave the insurgent city. The dispatch I had sent him is no longer there, and after many detours it finally reached Bologna, where Mr. Giri had withdrawn to another town; I have quite recently received from him, by courier, the reply he had addressed to the Chevalier Giri, Director of Police of Bologna, and I shall hasten to transmit a copy of it to you.

Meanwhile, until the authorities are reinstalled at their posts in Bologna, all judicial proceedings have been suspended, which explains the delay in replying. I hope that, thanks to the new measures taken, the proceedings will be able to resume their normal course and reach a prompt conclusion.

I regret that unforeseen circumstances have come to hinder a procedure that was progressing well, and I ask Your Excellency to believe that, for my part, I have neglected none of the means at my disposal to hasten the outcome of this affair.

I avail myself, etc. I have the honor to be,
Your Excellency’s most humble and most obedient servant,

Spitzer
