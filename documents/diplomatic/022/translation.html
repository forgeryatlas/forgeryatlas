His Excellency Fuad Pasha to Rustem Bey in Turin
11 April 1858
No. 1387

I have received your report No. 256, by which you communicate to me the translation of the copy that was transmitted to you with a note from the Sardinian Ministry of Foreign Affairs, containing the report of the examining magistrate on the results of the investigation into the case of the forgers named Venanzi, Varallo, and their associates. These new documents have likewise been communicated, for its information, to the Imperial Ministry of Police.

As no evidence has been established regarding the guilt of Varallo’s concubine, you did well not to oppose her release.

Please accept, etc.
