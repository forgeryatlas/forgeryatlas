Turin, 12 February 1861

To His Highness Aali Pasha Acting Minister of Foreign Affairs in Constantinople

No. G. 975
No. S. 196

Your Highness,

I have received the telegraphic dispatch which Your Highness was kind enough to send me, dated the 10th of this month, by which Your Highness informs me that a formal request has already been made to the authorities in Sicily, who have referred it to Count Cavour, concerning the reciprocal communication between the courts of the two countries of the records relating to the investigation of the case of the counterfeiters recently arrested in Messina, as well as the transmission of the correspondence seized from one of the offenders to the Imperial Ministry of Police.

Your Highness invites me to take the necessary steps to obtain a prompt and satisfactory resolution.

In accordance with the instructions of Your Highness, I immediately hastened to take the necessary steps with the Royal Ministry of Foreign Affairs, and I hope soon to be able to inform Your Highness of a satisfactory result.

Deign, Your Highness, to accept the assurances of my highest and most respectful consideration, with which I have the honor to be,

Of Your Highness,
the most humble
and most obedient servant

Rüstem
