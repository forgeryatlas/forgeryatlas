His Excellency Fuad Pasha to Rüstem Bey, Chargé d’Affaires in Turin

March 16, 1859, No. 2253

I have taken note of your report of February 24, No. 450, as well as of the translation
enclosed therein of the two documents which were addressed to you by the Apostolic Nunciature in Turin concerning the two counterfeiters of Bologna.

In reply to your request for details, I refer you to my dispatch of the 2nd of this month, No. 2200, and I inform you of the measures taken by the Imperial Government to ensure prompt and direct action in this matter.

I request you to communicate to the Nunciature the information that has been placed at your disposal.

Accept, etc.

S. E. Fuad Pacha à Rüstem Bey, Chargé d’Affaires à Turin

Translation

His Excellency Fuad Pasha to Rüstem Bey, Chargé d’Affaires in Turin

March 16, 1859, No. 2254

I have received and forwarded to the Imperial Ministry of Police your report of March 1, No. 453, concerning the arrest of the counterfeiters of Bologna, as well as the copy of the letters
addressed to you by the Cardinal Legate of Bologna and by Mr. the Regent of the Apostolic Nunciature in Turin, enclosed with this report.

I refer you to my previous dispatches on this matter regarding the information you request,
and I ask you to accept, Mr. Chargé d’Affaires, the assurances of my very distinguished consideration.

BOA HR.H. 169/2 (Ottoman Archives, Istanbul)

Translation

His Excellency Fuad Pasha to Rüstem Bey, Chargé d’Affaires of the Porte in Turin

March 16, 1859,No. 2257

I have received and communicated to the Imperial Ministry of Police your report dated March 3,
No. 454, relating to the conviction of the counterfeiters Venanzi and Varallo Pandolfini, and that rendered in absentia against Spadafora and Mayner Veiller.
