To His Excellency Fuad Pasha to Spitzer Effendi, Chargé d’Affaires at Naples

March 23, 1859
No. 2294

Monseigneur,

Mr. Tkiades, to whom you will kindly forward the enclosed letter, has received orders to return to Constantinople via Venice and Athens. You will take care to send him the sum of three thousand francs, which the correspondent of Mr. Alléon is authorized to place at your disposal for the travel expenses of this employee.

In the event that the authorities have not yet reached a final decision on the Bologna affair, you will explain that the continuation (of the matter) requires that the material evidence of the case be
transmitted to the Imperial Government.

Please accept, etc. etc.
