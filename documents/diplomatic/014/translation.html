His Excellency Fuad Pasha, Minister of Foreign Affairs
Constantinople

Monseigneur,

I have the honor to enclose herewith, for Your Excellency, a copy of a note from the Sardinian Ministry of Foreign Affairs, by which it transmits to me a copy of the report of the examining magistrate on the results of the investigation into the case of the counterfeiters Venanzi, Varallo, and their associates.

As the report was in Italian, I have translated it into French and likewise have the honor of submitting this translation herewith to Your Excellency.

Upon reviewing these documents, Your Excellency will see that the most convincing evidence of guilt has emerged against the accused, with the sole exception of Varallo’s concubine, Pandolfini, named Biancardi, against whom no evidence could be established. The Ministry of Justice therefore proposes that she be released.

After carefully examining the documents in the case file, I have found no reason to oppose the execution of this decision.

I have, however, instructed our prosecutor, Mr. Avoué Vayra, to request from the office of the Fiscal Advocate some clarifications on various points of the report which did not appear satisfactory to me, and I reserve the right to take the necessary measures once I have obtained the requested clarifications.

I have the honor to be,
Monseigneur,
Your Excellency’s
most humble and obedient servant,

Rüstem
