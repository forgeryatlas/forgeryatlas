His Excellency Fuad Pasha to Spitzer Effendi, Chargé d’Affaires in Naples

March 16, 1859
No. 2266

Sir,

I have received and communicated to the Ministry of Police your two reports of February 21 and 28 last, Nos. 147 and 149, concerning the manufacture of counterfeit paper money in Bologna.

While thanking you for the account you have provided in these reports, I take this opportunity to renew to you, Sir, the assurance of my highest consideration.

(marginal note)
I remember to send you the Caimes of 20 piastres (new) and of 10 piastres (old) that you requested.
