Milan, 22 February 1860

No. 701

Monseigneur,

Having heard that some of the individuals whom we had arrested in Bologna on charges of manufacturing counterfeit caimes had been released, I addressed myself to one of the principal bankers of that city, Mr. Bignami, whom I had known in Turin, in order to ask him to provide me with precise information on this matter. I have just received his reply: he informs me that the authorities of Bologna are bitterly complaining about the prolonged delays caused by the authorities of Constantinople in transmitting the documents that had been requested and which the Public Prosecutor considers necessary in order to bring the trial to a conclusion and secure the conviction of the guilty parties.

The Minister, Mr. Chiesi, has told me that the caimes and the other documents requested from Constantinople constitute indispensable pieces of evidence and that without them the trial cannot be concluded. In the meantime, as the accused could not be kept indefinitely in prison, they are currently under house arrest, and it is to be feared that if the requested documents from Constantinople are further delayed, they will be released.

It is now about four months since I had the honor of transmitting to Your Excellency the request of the authorities of Bologna, and several times since then I have taken the liberty of reminding them of this matter; and I frankly confess that I cannot understand the cause of the delay with which the Police of Constantinople are sending us the requested documents.

I must therefore once again request Your Excellency to kindly make renewed representations to His Excellency the Minister of Police in order to hasten the dispatch of these documents, regarding which I have already had the honor of submitting a memorandum.

I expect from one day to the next to receive a new request from the authorities of Bologna, and I shall be greatly embarrassed in replying to them.

I have the honor to be, Monseigneur, Of Your Excellency,
the very humble and very obedient servant,

Rüstem
