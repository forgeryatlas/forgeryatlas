His Excellency Fuad Pasha to Rustem Bey, Turin

March 31, 1858
No. 1336

Further to my dispatch no. 1324,

I am sending you herewith a new certified copy of the interrogations that Calvocoressi and Brescianini have recently undergone at the Ministry of Police, and which you will use as you deem most appropriate.

Please accept the assurance of my distinguished consideration.
