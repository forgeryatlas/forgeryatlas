His Excellency Fuad Pasha to Mr. Hosford Smith, Consul of the Sublime Porte in New York

November 17, 1858
No. 1815

Registered

Sir Consul,

I have received the report that you sent me dated October 8 last, concerning the counterfeit caimes that Mr. Harrison is said to have produced in New York, and I am grateful for the details you have provided on this matter.

As for the course of action that you must follow in this regard, I hasten to inform you that I have already given my instructions to the Ambassador of the Sublime Porte in London, and it only remains for you to conform to them strictly.

Please accept, Sir Consul, the assurance of my highest consideration.
