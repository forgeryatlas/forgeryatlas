Ministry of Foreign Affairs
Turin, 1 May 1860

Sir Chargé d’Affaires,

I hastened to recommend to the Department of Justice the request that you expressed to me in your note dated the 26th of this month, and I am pleased to inform you that the documents you had requested have been found.

In hastening to forward to you herewith the said documents—namely, the photographs which you had kindly submitted on 14 December 1859, and two authenticated copies of judicial acts relating to the proceedings instituted at Bologna against individuals accused of manufacturing Turkish paper money—I beg you, Sir Chargé d’Affaires, to accept, etc.

For the Minister, The Secretary General, (Signed) Carutti

Rüstem Bey Chargé d’Affaires of the Sublime Porte in Turin
