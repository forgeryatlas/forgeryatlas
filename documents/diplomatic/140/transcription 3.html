BOA HR.H. 114/2 (Ottoman Archives, Istanbul)

OTTOMAN CONSULATE NEW YORK.

New York March 15th 1859

His Excellency Fuad Pasha, Minister of Foreign Affairs &c &c, Constantinople

Excellency

I have the honor to acknowledge the receipt of Your Excellency’s despatch of the 9th Ultimo, through H. E. the Ambassador of the Imperial Government at London, conveying to me the satisfaction which the Imperial Government has felt at the promptness evinced by me, in the seizure of the false Caimes, and the various implements with which they were made.

I beg to offer to the Imperial Government my grateful acknowledgements for this gratifying assurance. I have but done my duty, as its Consul here yet the expression of approval, from a source so distinguished, affords me great pleasure

With the highest consideration
I have the honor to be Your Excellency’s Obedient Servant

S. Hosford Smith
Consul
