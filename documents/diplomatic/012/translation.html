His Excellency Fuad Pasha to Rüstem Bey
in Turin

March 17, 1858

No. 1287

I have received your report dated February 25, No. 226, concerning the individuals whom I had indicated to you in my telegraphic dispatch of February 10 as being implicated in a significant case of counterfeit caimes.

I hasten to express to you, in reply, the full satisfaction of the Sublime Porte with the zeal you have shown in pursuing this matter, and I fully approve the decision you have taken to constitute yourself as a civil party in the name of the Imperial Government in order to exercise a legitimate influence in the proceedings instituted against the accused.

The expenses which you may incur in the interest of this procedure will be reimbursed to you as soon as you submit to me a report in due time.

Please accept, etc.
