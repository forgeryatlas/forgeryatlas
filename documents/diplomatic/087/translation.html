His Excellency Fuad Pasha to Rüstem Bey, Turin
December 28, 1859
No. 3485

Counterfeit money

Monseigneur,

I have received your report of July 7 (No. 562), as well as the accounts that were submitted to us by Mr. Lecchio, the lawyer who pleaded before the courts on behalf of the Imperial Government in the case of the counterfeiters, and by Mr. Varya, the solicitor who acted as our representative in this matter.

I hasten to inform you, in reply, that in accordance with the decision, instructions have been given to Mr. Alleon to place at your disposal the sum of one thousand four hundred and five francs and sixty-five centimes, representing the total of the aforementioned accounts, which I transmit to you herewith in accordance with your request, and which you will return to us once they have been duly settled.

Please accept…
