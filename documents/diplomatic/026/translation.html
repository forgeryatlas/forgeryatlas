His Excellency Mahmoud Pasha Rüstem Bey, Chargé d’Affaires of the Sublime Porte in Vienna, temporarily in Paris
Registered

Sir Chargé d’Affaires,

I have received and immediately communicated to the Minister of Police the report which you sent me, dated June 25, No. 318, concerning the trial of the individual named Bondesio before the Sardinian courts, and I shall hasten to forward to you, as soon as they are received, the documents which you requested and which the said Ministry has asked me to communicate.

Please accept, Sir Chargé d’Affaires, the assurance of my distinguished consideration.
