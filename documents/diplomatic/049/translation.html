His Excellency Fuad Pasha, Minister of Foreign Affairs of His Imperial Majesty the Sultan

No. 158
Naples, March 14, 1859

Excellency,

I have just received a letter from the Director of Police in Bologna confirming Mr. Tkiades’s illness and informing me that he is nevertheless receiving all the care required. He further informs me that all the accused have been arrested with the exception of one individual, who has managed to evade the search of the authorities, and that the material evidence is now in the hands of the Police.

He again requests samples of genuine Caimes and of counterfeit Caimes, which I shall take care to send him as soon as Your Excellency is pleased to have them forwarded to me.

Another letter which has reached me at the moment of closing this dispatch informs me that Mr. Tkiades has entered into convalescence.

I have the honor, Excellency, to be, with the deepest respect,

Your Excellency’s
most humble and most obedient servant,

(signed) Spitzer
